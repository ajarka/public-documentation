Value Flows
💳

Donor → Platform → Merchant LIVE

Donor pays platform fee per tree (Silver $1 · Gold $2 · Platinum $3/yr). Platform deducts operational cost, remainder goes to merchant for tree stewardship. Payment via Midtrans (IDR) or Solana (SOL).

🔮

Platform → Solana NFT Q3 2026

Gold & Platinum service tiers unlock NFT ownership — transferable blockchain proof on Solana. Infrastructure & wallet auth live; on-chain minting activating H2 2026.

📊

Corporate → ESG Reports LIVE

Platinum corporate packages — bulk tree adoption with verified GIS coordinates, periodic surveillance reports (2x/year), and certificate bundles for annual ESG/sustainability reporting.

🤖

Bot Tira SaaS → Merchant LIVE

AI chatbot powered by Google Gemini with function-calling against live platform data. Merchant SaaS subscription — engages donors, answers tree queries, tracks conversation analytics. Already live on staging.

🌿

Merchant Campaign → Fundraising LIVE

Merchants launch campaigns with custom pricing (Regular vs. Campaign fee tier). Platform earns per-adoption fee. Donors browse and filter by campaign, merchant, region, or tree type.

💰

Carbon Credits → Future Revenue 2027

All adoptions generate real-time CO₂ tracking per species. Gold & Platinum tiers accumulate tradeable carbon credits. VCS certification targeted 2027 — enabling IDX Carbon exchange integration.
